---
metaTitle: Popover
metaDescription: Floating element for displaying rich content, triggered by a button.
---

```jsx live=true
<Popover.Root>
  <Popover.Trigger>
    <Button variant="solid">Open</Button>
  </Popover.Trigger>
  <Popover.Content style={{ width: 300 }}>
    <Text size="2" mb="3" as="p">
      Jan Tschichold was a German calligrapher, typographer and book designer.
      He played a significant role in the development of graphic design in the
      20th century.
    </Text>
    <Popover.Close>
      <Button variant="solid" size="1">
        Share <Share2Icon />
      </Button>
    </Popover.Close>
  </Popover.Content>
</Popover.Root>
```

## API Reference

This component inherits the props from the [Radix Popover](/primitives/docs/components/popover) primitive.

### Root

Contains all the parts of a popover.

### Trigger

Wraps the control that will open the popover.

### Content

Contains content to be rendered in the open popover. This component is based on the `div` element.

### Close

Wraps the control that will close the popover.

## Examples

### With inset content

Use the [Inset](/themes/docs/components/inset) component to align content flush with the sides of the popover.

```jsx live=true
<Popover.Root>
  <Popover.Trigger>
    <Button variant="solid">View details</Button>
  </Popover.Trigger>
  <Popover.Content>
    <Grid columns="1fr 1fr" style={{ width: 400 }}>
      <div>
        <Text as="p" size="3">
          Jan Tschichold was a German calligrapher, typographer and book
          designer. He played a significant role in the development of graphic
          design in the 20th century.
        </Text>
      </div>
      <Inset side="right" pl="current">
        <img
          src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1479030160180-b1860951d696?&auto=format&fit=crop&w=1200&q=80"
          style={{ objectFit: 'cover', width: '100%', height: '100%' }}
        />
      </Inset>
    </Grid>
  </Popover.Content>
</Popover.Root>
```
